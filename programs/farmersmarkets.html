How Our Markets Work

Our seasonal markets bring affordable, in-season produce to neighborhoods while supporting regional farms. We welcome multiple payment methods to keep food access equitable.

Season: Late spring – early fall
Payments: Cash, credit, SNAP/EBT, HIP, WIC, Senior FMNP
Access: Centrally located, ADA-friendly, near bus routes

Market Schedule

Downtown Fitchburg: Thursdays, 3–6 PM — arts & kids’ activities

Leominster Common: Saturdays, 9 AM–1 PM — chef demos monthly

Mobile Pop-Ups: Rotating locations — follow social for updates

EBT & HIP welcome. Spend SNAP and earn extra fruits & veggies with HIP.
